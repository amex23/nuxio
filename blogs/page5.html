The Complete Conversion Rate Optimization Guide for WordPress Websites




Having a leaky sales funnel is one of the prominent problems that every business may face today. If you’re one of those who want to find funnel drop-offs and fix the problem but don’t know where to start, that’s where conversion rate optimization comes in. It’s a way to increase the number of people who complete your desired action to get more out of existing traffic and leads. As a result, you generate more revenue and boost your conversion rate.

In this guide, we delve into what CRO is, what’s the process in WordPress, and how to increase website conversion rate.

Note: Improving website conversion rate is an ongoing process. If you want to make sure that whether you deliver an outstanding user experience that will lead to increased conversion rates or not, you need a behavior analytics tool like WatchThemLive. This tool helps you understand how your users move through your website and what’s distracting them from completing your goal; so you can fix your website’s pain points and ultimately improve website conversion rate.

What Is Conversion Rate Optimization?
Conversion rate optimization (CRO) is an evolutionary process of increasing conversion rate by persuading more visitors to complete your desired action. This desired action can be purchasing a product, signing up for a service, or clicking on a link. You might know how to make a website professionally, but if you can’t work on CRO, it will be hard to grow.

Why CRO Is Important What Is CRO Process In WordPress? WordPress is a platform that makes your conversion rate optimization process easier by managing your content. Here are the steps you need to take for the CRO of WordPress websites:

1. Identify Pages You Need to Optimize for Conversion The first step you need to take is to identify which elements on your website need optimization. Website optimization is important because it highly impacts your Google ranking. So if you want Google to make you visible among the millions of websites, you must optimize your website. Focus on the elements below to improve your ranking:

Keywords
Images
URL structure
Meta descriptions
Page titles
